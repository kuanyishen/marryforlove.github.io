Marry For Love, Not for Violence

Our mission is to encourage domestic violence victims to speak up, increase knowledge on rights against domestic violence, promote domestic violence support services and change the society’s attitude towards domestic violence.

“Marry for Love, Not for Violence” project was established in 2022 in Thailand. Unfortunately, Thailand is one of the top countries with high levels of domestic violence. According to the surveys 75% of women suffer from domestic violence on an ongoing basis and the trend is deteriorating. 

One of the main reasons is lack of information about the domestic violence legislation, support available for victims of domestic violence, and low level of awareness of the population overall about this problem. 

Violence is the problem, not the solution.

Our efforts are aimed to provide necessary information for the victims of domestic violence in a simple and accessible form. Here you can find sources of financial support, shelters, lawyers, psychological support, and learn new professions. 

Moreover, we realize that domestic violence victims often need support to achieve financial independence. 

Thus, we provide a wide range of short training sessions to obtain new skills to be able to start earning a certain level of income. 

“Marry for Love, Not for Violence” conducts informational sessions to enhance the awareness of people about domestic violence. 

Our organization collects donations for support of the victims of domestic violence. We have helped many women and children to start a new life free of violence, and we believe the best is yet to come.
